# Heart of Life

A simple heart progression system inspired by Lifesteal, designed for single-player Minecraft.  
Lose hearts when you die. Regain them by crafting and using heart-based items.

---

## Features

### 1. Permanent Heart Loss on Death
Every death removes **1 permanent heart**.  
You can never drop below **1 heart**.

---

### 2. Recover Hearts Through Crafting
Restore lost hearts by crafting **Heart Fragments** and turning them into a **Heart of Life**.

#### Heart Fragment Recipe Unlock
Unlocked automatically once you have:
- Totem of Undying
- Netherite Scrap
- Diamond

#### Heart of Life Recipe Unlock
Unlocked automatically once you have:
- Heart Fragment
- Heart of the Sea

**Using a Heart of Life:**
- Grants **+1 permanent heart**
- Applies **Regeneration I (5s)**
- Spawns heart particles
- Caps at **20 hearts total**

---

## 3. Custom Creative Tab
All Heart of Life items appear inside their **own custom creative category**.

---

## 4. Advancements
Includes themed advancements for progression:
- Acquiring a fragment  
- Crafting a Heart of Life  
- Using a heart  
- Reaching max hearts  
- Dropping to one heart  
- Collecting many hearts  

---

## Installation
1. Download the mod JAR.  
2. Place it in your `mods` folder (Fabric).  
3. Launch Minecraft.

---
